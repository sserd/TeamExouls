About Us
We are a group of university students persuing undergraduate and graduate studies from different parts of the country. The thing that brought us all together was our love for Astrophysics and research. During our 6-week internship at SSERD, we developed a modified theoretical modelling and data analysis techniques in order to further analyze the phase relation of transit timing and duration variations induced by possible exomoons.
